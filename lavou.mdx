## The Challenge

Create a brand identity for a first-generation artisanal bakery that communicates craft, warmth, and premium quality — without leaning on tired bakery clichés.

## Approach

We developed a logo system where product illustration and wordmark are inseparable. The donut isn't decoration — it's embedded in the name itself, making the brand instantly recognisable and entirely unique.

## The Outcome

Lavou launched with a complete visual identity that works equally well on Instagram, a printed menu, or a delivery box sticker.

> "Minimal but warm. Elegant, not overly decorative. Premium but approachable."
> — Lavou Brand Guidelines
